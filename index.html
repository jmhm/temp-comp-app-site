Walden's Student Companion App is now available

We are excited to release our new mobile app for all Walden students. The Walden University Student Companion App gives current students the opportunity to connect to important information around student life at Walden from your mobile device.

Note: : The Companion App offers easy access to information, however it does not replace or duplicate the classroom experience. Assignments and coursework can only be completed on your desktop at this time.
